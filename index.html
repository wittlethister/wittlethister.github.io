It's good if you already have products in mind. In case you have no idea what to sell, a list of products is provided inside this training program.

 Aside from the start-up capital, what else do I need to start this business?

You will be needing the following: desktop or laptop, stable internet connection, and a printer.

 Is there a money-back guarantee?

No. We already run the first batch, no one has requested a full refund of their enrollment fee. We are confident with the quality of teaching we offer.

 Can I do this business even if I'm based overseas?

Yes you can, as long as you can access the Seller Center but you need to have a trusted person here in the Philippines to fulfill the orders.

Logistics could be a little challenging but we have found solutions to make this business possible for sellers residing outside Metro Manila.

Pre-recorded videos are released on the first day of classes (Saturday) and live group training is scheduled on the second day (Sunday). Weekly group coaching is also scheduled every Saturdays.

None. We find group coaching more effective so we can all learn from each other's perspective and experiences.
